Books

Not to be confused with the temporary weapon, Book

Details

44 Subscriber Books were released on Feb 2016.

  • Books are Non-tradeable.
  • They cannot stack with other books.
  • Each book is unique - you can only obtain 1 copy of each book.
  • A book is a one time use item with a 31 day "book only" cooldown period, during which you have either a special effect/bonus or obtain stats at the end of. A book can only be reread via use of Book : Memories And Mammaries or a Ladso Eye.
  • Once used, a book will leave a non-useable copy of itself in your inventory for display purposes only.
  • At minimum will take you 3 years, 269 days to use all 44 books.
  • Being a Subscriber gives you the highest chance of obtaining one each month as a Subscriber Reward.
  • If you do not receive a book during one month of your subscriber rewards, the odds for receiving one the following month are increased, and vice-versa.
  • There is a very small chance of finding a book in the City.
  • Books are purchasable with mission credits if available as your mission rewards.
    • Books Tracker/Tutorial - Forum tracking book costs
  • The following books "The Hamburglars' Guide To Crime" and "Duke - My Story" are unavailable to currently obtain.
